About CoffeShop

Coffee beans

Raw coffee beans or green coffee beans are beans from ripe coffee cherries that have been removed from the pulp and shell. The raw coffee beans have been sorted and dried to meet market quality standards. Generally the coffee cherries that are harvested are red berries that ripen on the tree.

Coffee

Coffee is a drink made from brewed coffee beans that have been roasted and ground into powder. Coffee is one of the commodities in the world that is cultivated in more than 50 countries. Two species of coffee trees are generally known, namely Robusta Coffee (Coffea canephora) and Arabica Coffee (Coffea arabica).

Coffee cup

coffee cup is a place to serve coffee after going through the processing of coffee beans into coffee.
